Chicken Divan⏲ aprox. 45 minutes

Needed:

  • 1-2 lbs chicken breast
  • 1 1/2 bunches broccoli
  • 1 onion
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 2 cans cream of celery soup
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1/2 can milk
  • Curry powder
  • Cheddar cheese(1-2 packages)
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Butter

Step One: Chop the onion and cook it with the chicken breasts. Shred the chicken once cooked.

Step Two: Steam the broccoli florets until tender.

Step Three: In a bowl, mix the cream of chicken soup, cream of celery soup, mayo, and milk. Stir until smooth.

Step Four: In a baking dish, layer the steamed broccoli on the bottom, then the shredded chicken on top.

Step Five: Pour the soup mixture evenly over the chicken and broccoli until fully covered.

Step Six: Sprinkle cheddar cheese generously, then a light layer of curry powder.

Step Seven: Top with bread crumbs and dot with small pieces of butter.

Step Eight: Cover with foil and bake at 350Â°F for 30 minutes until bubbling. Remove foil and bake another 15 minutes.

Step Nine: Serve hot over rice.
